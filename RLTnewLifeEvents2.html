Yes. Upon completion of the probationary period, full-time permanent and 1 year or above contract employees are entitled to marriage leave of 3 working days on their first legal marriage. Marriage leave can only be taken once during an employee's employment with Ralph Lauren.

The marriage leave must be taken within 6 months of the official marriage registration date and the application must be supported by a copy of the marriage certificate.

Within 3 months (i.e. 90 days) from marriage date, you have to update marital status (Personal -> Personal Information -> Edit) and dependent's information (Actions -> View Dependents -> Add) in Workday. Please also provide a copy of the foreign legal documents and the dependent's passport for verification.

Within 3 months (i.e. 90 days) from the marriage date, you have to update your marital status (Personal -> Personal Information -> Edit) and spouse's (or partner's) information (Actions -> View Dependents -> Add) in Workday. Please also provide a copy of the legal marriage certificate and spouse's (or partner's) HKID copy for verification. Please provide a passport copy if your spouse or partner does not have an HKID. After that, you will receive an email from our vendor CXA in order to select the plan of your choice. Complete the selection and process within the given deadline. Manulife will then be able to issue the medical card for your spouse or partner. It usually takes about two weeks for a new card to arrive.

If you wish to change your Life Insurance's beneficiary, please return the Beneficiary Nomination Form to your P&D Business Partner. You should also update the beneficiary's information in Workday.

Please inform your P&D Partner of the change. In Workday, update your marital status (Personal -> Personal Information -> Edit) and delete the dependent's information (Actions -> View Dependents -> Delete). After that, CXA will prorate the flex dollars balance and Manulife will terminate your spouse's (or partner's) medical insurance coverage

If you wish to remove or change your Life Insurance's beneficiary, please return the Beneficiary Nomination Form to your P&D Business Partner. You should also update the beneficiary's information in Workday.
