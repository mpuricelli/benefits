In the event of child birth or adoption, Permanent Full-Time and Part-Time employees after 1 year of service with the Company are entitled to Ralph Lauren Paid Parental Leave, except those who are serving notice period.

In addition to any statutory entitlements Ralph Lauren provides all new parents, who are Full-time or Part-time employees with 12 months continuous service, with 4 weeks paid parental leave.
For Primary Carers with a minimum of 2 years’ service this benefit is extended to 12 weeks paid parental leave.
