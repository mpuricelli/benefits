Congratulations & Condolences Allowances

You will receive company gifts on the following special occasions:

Marriage: a marriage gift of HK$1,000 for an employee's first legal marriage

Birth of Child:a birth gift of HK$800 for an employee's new born baby

Illness: at Business/Department manager's discretion, a get-well gift (i.e. a bouquet of flowers or fruit basket) of HK$500 may be sent if an employee or their spouse or children is hospitalized

Bereavement: at Business/Department manager's discretion, a condolence wreath of HK$1,000 and token of HK$1,000 may be sent in the event of the death of an employee's immediate family member (i.e. parents, spouse, child, parents-in-law, brother, sister, and grandparents.)
